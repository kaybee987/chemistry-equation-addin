Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 13, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Chemistry Equation Editor Microsoft Word add-in ("the App") handles user information. The App is provided by FlaskAI.

Information We Collect

The Chemistry Equation Editor does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information from its users. The App operates entirely within your local Microsoft Word environment.

How the App Works

The Chemistry Equation Editor is a client-side application that runs within the Microsoft Word taskpane. All equation building, formula rendering, and document insertion operations are performed locally on your device. Specifically:

  • No user data is sent to any external server
  • No account registration or login is required
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No analytics or telemetry data is collected
  • No document content is read, stored, or transmitted outside of your local Word application

Data Storage

The App does not store any data outside of the current Word document session. Equations and formulas you create are inserted directly into your Word document using the standard Office JavaScript API. No data is retained by the App between sessions.

Third-Party Services

The Chemistry Equation Editor does not integrate with or send data to any third-party services, APIs, or analytics platforms. The App's static files are hosted on GitHub Pages, which may log standard web server access information (such as IP addresses) as described in GitHub's Privacy Statement.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
